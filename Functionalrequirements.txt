1. The system can book appointments for patients with a dentist, surgery location, date, and time.

2. The system sends a confirmation email after an appointment is booked.

3. Dentists can view their scheduled appointments.

4. Patients can view, cancel, or change their appointments.

5. The system prevents invalid bookings, such as more than 5 appointments per dentist per week or booking for patients with unpaid bills older than 12 months.
